About Us

Welcome to Ukrainians in South West Kerry, a dedicated platform that celebrates and supports the vibrant Ukrainian community residing in this beautiful region of Ireland. Our mission is to create a space where people can come together, share their experiences, and stay connected with their heritage and local events, while fostering friendships and a sense of belonging.

Established in 2021, our website has grown to become an essential resource for Ukrainians in South West Kerry and beyond. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities that come with living in a new country, which is why we strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. Our team is made up of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, each bringing their own expertise and enthusiasm to the table.

At Ukrainians in South West Kerry, we provide a wide range of content, including local community news, world and Irish news, cultural events, and personal stories. We also offer practical information on living and working in the region, as well as resources to help newcomers integrate into their new surroundings. Our goal is to create a supportive community that fosters a strong connection to our shared Ukrainian heritage and contributes to the rich tapestry of life in South West Kerry.

We encourage you to join our community, whether you are of Ukrainian descent, have an interest in Ukrainian culture, or simply want to learn more about this fascinating part of the world. Explore our website, get to know our team, and find out how you can get involved in our events and initiatives. We look forward to welcoming you to Ukrainians in South West Kerry!
