Paws for Applause:
A Local Dog Elected as Mayor of Glenbeigh, a Small Town in South West Kerry

Bark the Vote: How a Furry Four-Legged Friend Became the Leader of a Charming Irish Town

By Joe Bidoni | May 16, 2023


In a surprising turn of events that has captured both hearts and headlines, the residents of Glenbeigh, a small town in South West Kerry, have elected a local dog as their mayor. The lovable Labrador Retriever named Finn, known for his friendly demeanor and wagging tail, has triumphed in the most recent mayoral election, leaving his two-legged opponents in the dust.

The story began as a lighthearted joke among the townspeople, who were growing weary of the same old political candidates and their predictable campaign promises. In a moment of collective hilarity, the idea of nominating Finn as a mayoral candidate was floated – and the idea quickly took hold.

Finn's campaign, run by his devoted owner and a team of enthusiastic volunteers, focused on the simple yet powerful message of "love, loyalty, and belly rubs for all." With his adorable face and wagging tail, Finn quickly gained the support of Glenbeigh's residents, who were charmed by the prospect of a canine mayor.

On election day, the townspeople flocked to the polls, eager to cast their votes for the four-legged candidate. When the results were tallied, it was clear that Finn had won by a landslide, securing his place as the first dog mayor in Irish history.

Finn's mayoral duties, which include presiding over town meetings and attending community events, have been met with enthusiasm by both the new mayor and his constituents. With a wag of his tail, Finn brings a sense of joy and unity to the town, reminding residents of the simple pleasures in life.

While some may question the practicality of a canine mayor, the people of Glenbeigh are quick to point out the positive impact Finn has had on their community. His presence has brought people together, fostering a sense of camaraderie and shared purpose that transcends traditional politics.

As the tale of Mayor Finn spreads far and wide, other towns and cities around the world are taking notice, with some even considering the possibility of their own four-legged leaders. Whether this trend will continue remains to be seen, but for now, the residents of Glenbeigh are delighted with their furry mayor and the sense of joy he brings to their community.

In a world that often feels divided and contentious, the story of Finn and the town of Glenbeigh serves as a heartwarming reminder of the power of laughter, love, and a wagging tail to bring people together – no matter how surprising the circumstances.
